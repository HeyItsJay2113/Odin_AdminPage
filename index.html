Available Quests

Stormcall at Gullinbursti’s Gate

The golden boar of the gods stirs in the eastern meadows, vexed by the whispers of frost giants and shadowed creatures. Thor, raise Mjolnir high and shatter the icy chains that bind its path. Only by guiding the boar safely to the halls of Asgard can its fury be contained, lest the creature trample both mortal fields and sacred groves alike.

The Raven’s Secret Flight

Odin’s ravens have glimpsed a shadow creeping through the branches of Yggdrasil, a secret that might unravel the threads of fate. Seek the hidden path between the worlds, pierce the veils of mist, and uncover what the dark leaves whisper. Bring knowledge of the unseen, for the All-Father’s eyes are blind to nothing, and even a single shadow may tip the balance.

The Forge of Surtur

Surtur’s molten fire glimmers across the horizon, threatening the lands of Midgard with unbridled flame. Thor, venture into the burning plains of Muspelheim, wrest the molten secrets from the fire-lord’s grip, and quench the flames that seek to leap into mortal realms. Only your strength can preserve both forge and world from ruin, until the embers of chaos are once more subdued.

Jormungandr’s Tide

The great serpent coils beneath the rolling waves, restless and hungry, its movements sending tremors across the seas. Thor, take ship across the roaring waters, strike at its underbelly with thunder and steel, and bind its fury before the oceans rise to claim the shores. Delay not, for each passing tide strengthens the serpent and darkens the hearts of men with fear.

The Whispering Halls of Hel

From the icy corridors of Helheim, voices rise in sorrow and discontent, calling for reckoning among the living. Descend into the frozen halls, pierce the veils that separate the worlds, and guide the restless spirits back to their place. Only by honoring the balance of life and death can the realms remain secure, and the whispers of the lost be silenced.

The Frost Giant’s Gambit

Under the shimmering northern lights, frost giants plot a tide of stone and frost to sweep across the mortal lands. Thor, ride the storm with hammer in hand, shatter their cunning schemes, and scatter their host across frozen peaks. Should you falter, the giants’ icy hand may grasp the kingdoms of men, leaving only frost and shadow in their wake.
